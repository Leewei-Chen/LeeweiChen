The Kimpton Hotel- Taipei Daan

Taipei, Taiwan | 2017

Project Overview

The project is a luxury apartment renovation. Kimptom Hotel Group decided to make it a boutique hotel in Taipei with 129 guestrooms. The design took inspiration from Taipei's everyday and conveyed the idea of interior sanctuary through layered of wooden facades. Several studies of facades was conducted in the design process aiming to create a sense of calmness for vistors to enjoy a luxury journey in city Taipei. Full-
