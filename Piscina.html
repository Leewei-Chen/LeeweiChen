Piscina Mirabilis-Reuse the Roman Ruin

Shanghai, China | 2020

Project Overview

The project is to reuse this space as a Contemporary Art Museum. When facing Piscina Mirabilis, one of the reservoirs with the longest history, we hope to arouse people's attention to it and develop it into a public space integrating exhibition, catering and speech. The design concept is to continue the existing context, combine the existing structure, form an organic space, and finally inject vitality by visitors to make it great again.
